About us

Get To Know About Us

Transforming how citizens, cities, households, businesses consume and manage their wastes.

Trash point is a suitable and an innovative youth-led waste management company making it possible for households and organisations in Jos Plateau state, Nigeria to conveniently choose responsible consumption and recycle their wastes. To address the issue of overconsumption, indiscriminate waste and environmental pollution in Jos, we provide an integrated platform where anyone can easily request pickup or locate a drop-off center to recycle their wastes.

Join us, to be a part of the solution and not the polution.

Vision

TO be a leader in transitioning to a world without waste.

Mission

To enable communities leverage for growth and to foster a culture of responsible consumption.
